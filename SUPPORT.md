Having trouble with Wukong CLI? We're here to help!

Visit our [official documentation](https://docs.wukong.today) for:

- Configuration options
- Troubleshooting tips

## Community

Join our community to connect with other users:

- **Discord:** [discord.gg/wukong](https://discord.gg/wukong)
- **GitHub Discussions:** [Discussions](https://github.com/mutnpc/wukong-cli/discussions)

## Contact Support

For product support and account help:

- **Email:** [support@wukong.today](mailto:support@wukong.today)
- Include: OS, Node version, CLI version, and steps to reproduce

## Report a Bug

Use the built-in bug reporter:

```bash
wukong bug
```

Or file a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/mutnpc/wukong-cli/issues).

## Security Issues

If you believe you've found a security vulnerability, please see [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
